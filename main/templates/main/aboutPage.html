About Us
This is a Web Application for you if you are a tenant living in social housing in Southwark and Lambeth and being taken to court for rent arrears.
This app will help you find out whether you are eligible financially for legal aid.
The app will also show you local housing solicitor who provide legal aid.
The best possible outcome is you will be in contact with the solicitor before the day of your court hearing and discus if they will represent you in court.
It is important to remember legal aid is limited, and you may be eligible and still not get it.
This app is here to prompt you to go to court with the information you will input into this app.
This is the same if you are not eligible.
The information you provide can be printed and used on the day of your court hearing.
If there is a duty solicitor there, they can quickly review your information and if not, the judge will have all the information they need.
